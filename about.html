Hello, I am Gina, the founder and owner of ImaGINAtion Nails! I have always had a passion for painting nails and nail art. It wasn't until quarantine that I thought, "why not," and started selling my handmade designs in the form of press-on nails! Since March of 2021, my shop has grown tremendously, and I am so grateful for all the support and love! I hope to continue to produce nails that follow current trends and ignite the imagination within us all!
